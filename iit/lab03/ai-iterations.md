This document tracks the prompt engineering process and iteration history used to enhance my personal website for Lab 3b.

---

## Iteration 1 – Founder-style Redesign Direction

**Prompt Summary:**  
Requested a unique, inspiring, “Elon Musk / Bill Gates” style personal website. Required NO JavaScript and NO one-line CSS. Asked AI to enhance layout and overall visual identity while keeping it A-level appropriate.

**Result:**  
AI generated a cleaner, more modern homepage structure with stronger branding and section clarity.

**What I Learned:**  
The more specific I was about constraints (no JavaScript, no one-liner CSS), the better the output aligned with assignment rules.

---

## Iteration 2 – Navigation Behavior Improvement

**Prompt Summary:**  
Requested that clicking Resume open in a new tab to prevent user confusion.

**Result:**  
Implemented `target="_blank"` and `rel="noopener"` to improve usability without JavaScript.

**What I Learned:**  
AI can provide simple UX improvements, but I must evaluate whether the change improves or complicates navigation.

## Iteration 3 – Headshot Integration
